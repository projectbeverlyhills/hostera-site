A few things we've noticed

Pace

You dine out roughly twice a month, almost always after 7 PM — this reads as a considered evening out, not a quick bite between errands.

Radius

Most visits stay close to home — Silver Lake, the Arts District, a short drive down the coast. You favor depth over breadth in the neighborhoods you already trust.

Palate

Natural wine and open-fire cooking show up again and again — kitchens that let one good ingredient do most of the talking.
